My Research

My research focuses on understanding the impacts of land use/land cover change, global warming and human practices on terrestrail, and afreshwater ecosystems. By integrating remote sensing, machine learning, and process-based modeling, I aim to develop data-driven solutions for sustainable water and land management. My work has broad implications for improving agricultural resilience, guiding conservation policies, and supporting climate adaptation strategies. By bridging scientific research with real-world applications, my goal is to provide actionable insights that benefit both local communities and global sustainability efforts.
